    Grove Mail

    Our goal is to leverage IBM's Qiskit SDK to create a mailing service that delivers emails over a simulated quantum channel (Quantum One Time Pad), establishing secure communication by leveraging physical rules rather than mathematical methodologies or computational capabilities to offer security. This will eventually allow us to replace this channel with a real quantum channel.

    Topic Decription

    Currently, the Classical Open Systems Interconnection Model (OSI model) contains numerous levels. Using the Quantum OSI paradigm, we will condense these layers to email service and multi-party computing. Both the Sender and the Receiver will have an undefined number of local qubits while using GroveMail. They will also share a quantum channel (or will be entangled). As a result, both Sender and Receiver can operate and measure their local qubits and the quantum channel.

    Quantum State Exhange

    The quantum state exchange is a quantum communication task in which two users share their respective quantum information in an asymptotic situation.

    Quantum Teleportation

    Quantum teleportation is a process of transferring quantum information from a transmitter in one location to a recipient in another location.

    Quantum Superposition

    The characteristic of a quantum system that allows it to exist in multiple quantum states at the same time.

    Quantum One Time Pad

    A quantum one-time pad is a shared quantum state that enables two parties to communicate private messages or quantum states across a public quantum channel.
